My history and plans for future ABOUT ME

The Very Beginning

When I was a kid I used to be fond of simple video games my father and uncle showed me on their first PC. It is no uncommon knowledge that children at my age were fascinated about it but in my case there was always a difference. Not only was I interested in how to win the game but I was curious how the game worked and how it was made as well. Unfotunately the questions I have asked were never answered. Untill..

A Little Spark

Untill I started education in IT school. Long story short. In TLDR I thought that school will be able to show me how to build incredible websites or at least how to make games that would make my father proud of me.

Such a shame… you would think, but the education was not to be put into waste. Some of the teachers have shown me the direction I want to go and motivated me enough to take what I want when it comes to my life goals. What is more, they taught me that I should start learning by myself in order to achieve my destination. There was a time when the lessons started to be too easy for me…

Present...

Up to this point nothing has changed my perspective. I still want learn more. I’m testing new technologies and improving my knowledge to be able to create even more advanced projects. This is what makes me happy and proud at the same time. It does not matter if I fail. It is not important if someone will create something better because we all can learn from each other and begin to work on things that has never been done before and this makes me more motivated. Additionally, it allows me to push forward..

The nearest future

Right now I'm nothing more than beginner, but hey! This is where we all started, right? In the nearest future I want to be part of something bigger. I want to work with people that will help me improve and become someone better, as my teachers did. No matter how hard and exhausting this path will be!
