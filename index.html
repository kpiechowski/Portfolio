When I was a kid, I was fascinated about simple video games that my father and his brother showed me on thier first PC. Well...I guess every kid in my age was fascinating about it, but in my case there was a diffrence. Not only i was intrested how to beat the game, but also i was curious how the game works and how was made. Unfortunately those questions weren't answered.
Until...

A Little Spark

Until I started education in IT school. Long short story. In TLDR I thought that school will show me how to build incredible websites or at least games that will make my father proud.
Shame...you would think, but it wasn't in vain. Some teachers have shown me the directon and interested me enough to take everything in my own hands and start learning by myself. I was a time where lessons started to be too easy for me...

Present...

In this point, where I am right now nothing has been changed. I still want to learn more and more. I'm trying out new technologies and improve to create even more advance things and you know what... This is what makes me fun and proud at the same time. No matter if i fail or someone will create something better. This is a factor that makes me even more motivated and allows me to push forward.

Right now I'm nothing more than beginner, but hey! This is where we all started, right? In the nearest future I want to be part of something bigger. I want to work with people that will help me improve and become someone better, as my teachers did. No matter how hard and exhausting this path will be!
